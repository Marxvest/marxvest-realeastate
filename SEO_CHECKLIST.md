# Marxvest SEO Checklist

1. Deploy the latest version to Vercel.
2. Confirm `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` is set to the active production domain.
3. Open `/sitemap.xml` and confirm it loads successfully.
4. Open `/robots.txt` and confirm the sitemap URL is correct.
5. Add the site to Google Search Console.
6. Verify ownership using your preferred verification method.
7. Submit the sitemap in Google Search Console.
8. Inspect the homepage URL and confirm it is indexable.
9. Request indexing for the homepage if needed.
10. Inspect `/listings` and confirm the canonical URL is correct.
11. Inspect the major listing detail pages and confirm they are indexable.
12. Inspect `/blog` and the most important blog posts.
13. Monitor the Coverage or Indexing report for crawl issues.
14. Monitor the Performance report for search queries, CTR, and ranking growth.
